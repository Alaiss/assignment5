Explanation:

From the assignment document, I understood that the audio that I'm to make should be working together with a video later on. That's why I decided to record the audio of a story and later on supply it with still images. I added some background music that changes as the story progresses because the audio would sound very raw and uninteressting if it was just the sound of my voice, it's done to add a flavour to the recording (a lot of bloggers do it too) as well as to change the mood of the narrative.
I found a website with free to use and download audio recordings and picked out 4 music clips that I thought were best suited for my purpose. The first one is a happy and peaceful one, for the beginning. Second one has a bit of a dangerous sound, third one - heroic and a bit of a "ready-to-fight" kind of soundtrack, the fourth one - energetic and cheerful. With the help of Audacity as a tool, I mixed up all these recordings, plus the recording of my voice telling the story, in a single audio. It also helped me to get rid of some unnecessary sounds in my recording. Then, I used Win Movie Maker to make a video from my still images, after which I layed a final recording over it. As for the images, because of the kind of story I made up, I thought that it'd be easier to take the pictures using my camera instead of looking for them on the Internet.

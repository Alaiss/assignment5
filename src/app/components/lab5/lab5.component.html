My Video:

Explanation of your work process:

For this lab I took one of the videos that I had on my phone and decided to use it for this lab. I added a proper title screen as well as the subtitles and credits, I didn't forget to add the animated transitions in too. I addded a background music for both the title and credits. The soundtrack in the beginning fades out and the one at the end fades in. I also added subtitles for the video and an effect from video stabilization called "anti-shake", so that the shaking of the camera is a bit less noticeable Lastly, I trimmed the video a little bit.

your thoughts on video creation:

It was definitely a good experience and something I haven't done in a while. Making the subtitles, surprisingly, turned out ot be quite an easy work to do. The only thing that's bothered me was a quality decrease after the modification of the video. Otherwise, it was fine.
